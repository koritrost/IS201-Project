What is the Disney College Program?

  • Internship open to all majors

  • Provides work experience in customer service

  • Looks great on any resume

  • Emphasizes three areas
    1. Living
    2. Learning
    3. Earning

Why did I do the Disney College Program?

I did the Disney College Program, because I have always had a passion for Disney and I wanted the work experience on my resume. As I will be applying to BYU's EXDM program this summer, having experience with a company that is known for its experiences will give me an edge.


My Disney College Program Experience

On my DCP, I experienced the following:

  1. Worked in Magic Kingdom

    • Role classified as Fantasyland Stands

  2. Met Guests/Cast Members from around the World

  3. Developed Skills Applicable in all Work Environments
